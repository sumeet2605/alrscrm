# Sprint 2 Family Domain

## Purpose

Sprint 2 establishes ALRSCRM as a family-centric CRM. The `Family` aggregate is the primary record that future bookings, shoots, galleries, editing jobs, invoices, and delivery workflows should reference.

## Aggregate

`Family` is the aggregate root. It owns:

- Family members
- One address
- Service interests
- Soft-delete state

Tags are modeled independently through `FamilyTag` and `FamilyTagMapping` so they can become reusable segmentation metadata.

See `docs/Family_Aggregate_Diagram.md` for the aggregate diagram and
`docs/Domain_Event_Map.md` for the implementation event map.

## Business Rules

- Every family belongs to one organization and one branch.
- The branch must belong to the same organization as the family.
- `primary_contact_phone` is unique across active family records.
- `family_code` is generated by the backend in the format `ALS-000001`.
- Families are soft-deleted by setting `deleted_at`.
- Family members and service interests are replaced as aggregate-owned child collections during family update.

## Roles

- Owner: full family access across the organization.
- Branch Manager: create, view, edit, and delete families in their branch.
- Sales Executive: create, view, and edit families.
- Photographer: read-only.
- Editor: read-only.
- Customer Success: read-only.
- Super Admin: platform-wide access.

## Frontend Routes

- `/families`
- `/families/new`
- `/families/:familyId`
- `/families/:familyId/edit`

Create, edit, and delete controls are hidden from read-only roles. Backend RBAC remains the source of truth.
